Design a modern e-commerce website UI for an art brand called "craftco.lab".

The website sells:
- Paintings
- Custom Paintings
- Bookmarks
- Postcards

STYLE REQUIREMENTS:
- Minimal, soft, aesthetic, artistic vibe
- Feminine but elegant
- Clean layout similar to a modern jewellery e-commerce website
- Soft rounded corners
- Subtle shadows
- Large hero banner
- Professional product grid layout
- Minimal icons

COLOR THEME:
Use only these two shades:
1) Light Dusty Rose (#E6A8A8) for background sections
2) Dark Rose Pink (#D85C63) for buttons, highlights, borders, tags, footer, and banners

Do NOT use bright red.
Keep everything soft and cohesive.

LAYOUT STRUCTURE:

1) Header:
- Centered logo text: craftco.lab
- Navigation: Categories, Hot Deals, Store, Track Order, Returns/Exchange, Contact Us
- Right side icons: Search, User Login, Wishlist, Cart

2) Announcement Bar:
- Dark rose background
- White text
- Messages like: "Free Shipping Above ₹999 | Limited Edition Art Drops | Custom Paintings Available"

3) Hero Section:
- Large aesthetic banner
- Big heading: #ART COLLECTION
- Subtext: Handmade paintings & creative prints you'll love
- Soft artistic background
- Rounded edges

4) Category Grid Section:
4 large rounded cards with image placeholders:
- Paintings
- Custom Paintings
- Bookmarks
- Postcards

Hover effect: dark rose overlay

5) Featured Products Section:
- 4–6 product cards
- Image
- Title
- Price
- Add to Cart button (dark rose button with rounded edges)

6) Connect With Us Section:
Two rounded cards:
- Chat With Us
- Email Us

7) FAQ Section:
Accordion style
Rounded pink cards
Dark rose borders

8) Footer:
Dark rose background
4 columns:
- About craftco.lab
- Quick Links
- Store Policy
- Newsletter subscription field + Subscribe button

Design should look like a premium art brand, modern, clean, aesthetic and Instagram-friendly.

Make it desktop layout first.
